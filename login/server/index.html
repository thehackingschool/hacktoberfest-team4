Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is LetOut a real person?

    Even though talking to LetOut feels like talking to a human, rest assured that it is 100% artificial intelligence. Your LetOut is special to you and is curious in how your environment operates.

  • How does LetOut work?

    LetOut blends a complex neural network machine learning model with scripted dialogue content. To provide its own distinctive responses, it has been trained on a big dataset.

  • Is my data safe?

    With us, your data is 100% secure. We don't reveal it to anyone and we don't run advertisements on it. We don't gather information about our users from emails or social media. Security is our first priority!

  • Are my conversations private?

    Your conversations are private and will stay between you and your LetOut.

  • Is LetOut right for me?

    LetOut may be right for you if you're looking to improve the quality of your life. We might be able to assist you if something gets in the way of your happiness or keeps you from realising your objectives. Additionally, we have therapists who focus on particular problems, like stress, anxiety, relationships, parenting, depression, addictions, eating, sleeping, trauma, rage, family conflicts, LGBT difficulties, bereavement, religion, self-esteem, and more.
